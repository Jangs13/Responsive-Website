Welcome to Stylopedia !

Stylopedia is a newly launched service product of the company Stylopedia Tech Pvt Ltd. Stylopedia is an easy-to-use App by which customers can book online appointments, and make online payments at their favourite salons and beauty parlours. We are a bridge between the style outlets and the customers. We help our customers discover the nearest style/beauty outlets and give them an option to book their slot online so that they can save their time. We also provide the facility for making online payments to ensure hassle-free experiences for our customers.
